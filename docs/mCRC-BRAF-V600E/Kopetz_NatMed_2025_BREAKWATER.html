Abstract

EC is approved for previously treated BRAF V600E-mutant mCRC based on BEACON. The phase 3 BREAKWATER study investigated EC+mFOLFOX6 vs SOC in previously untreated BRAF V600E mCRC. BREAKWATER met the dual primary endpoint of ORR (EC+mFOLFOX6: 60.9%; SOC: 40.0%; OR 2.443; P=0.0008). Median DOR was 13.9 vs 11.1 months. First interim OS HR was 0.47.

Safety

Grade 3/4 AEs: 74.0% vs 61.0%. Grade 5: 4.3% vs 4.4%. Most common: nausea 51.1%, anemia 36.4%, diarrhea 34.2%.

Conclusions

BREAKWATER showed substantially improved clinical benefit with EC+mFOLFOX6 as first-line treatment for BRAF V600E-mutant mCRC.
