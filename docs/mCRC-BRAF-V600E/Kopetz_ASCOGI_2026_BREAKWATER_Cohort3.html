BREAKWATER: Primary Analysis of First-Line (1L) Encorafenib + Cetuximab (EC) + FOLFIRI in BRAF V600E-Mutant mCRC

Authors: Kopetz S, Wasan HS, Yoshino T, Kim TW, Eng C, Van Cutsem E, et al.
Presented at: ASCO GI 2026
Study: BREAKWATER Cohort 3 (Phase 3)
Note: This is a conference presentation. No peer-reviewed journal full text is available.

Study Design

  • EC+FOLFIRI arm: n=73
  • Control (FOLFIRI ± bevacizumab): n=74

Safety

The safety profile was consistent with the known profiles of each individual agent. No new safety signals were identified.

Conclusions

EC+FOLFIRI demonstrated a statistically significant improvement in ORR compared with FOLFIRI ± bevacizumab in first-line BRAF V600E-mutant mCRC. The results support EC + chemotherapy as a backbone for first-line treatment, with both mFOLFOX6 and FOLFIRI as viable chemotherapy partners.
